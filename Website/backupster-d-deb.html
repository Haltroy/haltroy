Steps

Here are the steps that you should take while installing Backupster to a Debian-based GNU/Linux distribution.

1

Confirm

Confirm your download by opening your termianl emulator of choice on the directory of your download and entering this command:
md5sum Backupster.deb
The output should be this code:
[[NO VERSION AVAILABLE]]
2

Install

Open the file and click "Install".
If you cannot install it this way, try doing it in console with this command:
sudo dpkg -i Backupster.deb
3

Done!

Just follow the on-screen instructions.
